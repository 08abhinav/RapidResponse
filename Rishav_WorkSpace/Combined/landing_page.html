Optimized Emergency Routing System

RapidResponse is an innovative Data Structures and Algorithms (DSA) project designed to optimize the pathfinding process for emergency vehicles.
In critical situations, time is of the essence, and the ability to navigate quickly and efficiently through traffic can make all the difference in saving lives.
This project leverages advanced DSA techniques to create a system that calculates the most optimal routes for emergency responders, taking into account real-time traffic data, road conditions, and other dynamic factors.
By focusing on both speed and accuracy, RapidResponse aims to improve emergency response times and enhance the overall effectiveness of emergency vehicle navigation systems.
